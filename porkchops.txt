Stovetop Pork Chops

Seasoning: Creole, Seasoned Salt, Onion Powder
			Spread evenly on both sides of each chop

Add oil and butter to a pan
Heat on high until oil is hot

Place chop(s) onto the pan and cook on high for 2 minutes
Flip chop(s) and cook on high for another 2 minutes

Flip chop(s) again and cook on medium heat for 10-15 minutes.
	Have the pan covered for this time.
	
Check to make sure they are cooked to perfection.

Enjoy.
